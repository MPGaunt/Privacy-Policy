# Puzzlefy Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

## Overview

Puzzlefy respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information Puzzlefy collects, how it is used, and what choices you have when using the app.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use Puzzlefy.

## Information We Collect

Puzzlefy may collect the following categories of information:

### Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:
- email address
- username
- account identifier provided through our authentication system

### User Content

When you create custom puzzles, we may collect:
- images you upload
- puzzle titles
- puzzle metadata such as piece count and creation date

### App Activity

When you use Puzzlefy, we may collect and store:
- saved puzzle records
- completion data
- leaderboard submissions
- premium entitlement status

### Purchase Information

If you subscribe to premium features, Apple may provide us with transaction and entitlement information needed to determine access. We do not receive your full payment card details.

### Advertising Data

If ads are enabled, advertising partners such as Google AdMob may collect device and usage data in accordance with their own policies.

### Device and Technical Information

We and our service providers may process limited technical data required to operate the app, such as:
- app version
- device type
- operating system version
- crash or error-related information

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- create and manage user accounts
- authenticate users
- save and sync puzzle content
- store and display user-created puzzles
- power leaderboard functionality
- restore premium access
- serve ads where applicable
- maintain app security, reliability, and abuse prevention
- improve the app and user experience

## Services and Third Parties

Puzzlefy currently relies on third-party services, which may process information as part of app operation:

- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Apple App Store / StoreKit
- Google AdMob
- Pexels, for puzzle artwork sourcing where enabled

These services operate under their own privacy terms and policies.

## User-Generated Content

If you upload images or create custom puzzles, that content may be stored on our backend services so that it can be saved, reopened, or managed within the app.

You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload and use any image you submit to Puzzlefy.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:
- provide the app
- maintain your account
- preserve saved content
- comply with legal obligations
- resolve disputes
- enforce our policies

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and whether you delete your account or content.

## Your Choices

Depending on how you use Puzzlefy, you may be able to:
- update or rename saved puzzle content
- delete saved puzzle content
- stop using the app at any time
- request account-related changes where supported

You may also manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

## Children’s Privacy

Puzzlefy is not intended for children under 13, or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction, without parental or guardian involvement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information processed through Puzzlefy. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International Use

If you use Puzzlefy from outside the country where our service providers operate, your information may be processed in other jurisdictions.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date above and publish the revised policy.
